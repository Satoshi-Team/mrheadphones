What to Look For in Budget Headphones

Sound Quality

Look for headphones with balanced frequency response, clear mids, and decent bass. Avoid models that are overly bass-heavy or tinny-sounding.

Build Quality

Check for sturdy construction, comfortable padding, and durable materials. Budget headphones should still last several years with proper care.

Comfort

Look for adjustable headbands, soft ear cushions, and lightweight design. You should be able to wear them for hours without discomfort.

Connectivity

Consider whether you need wired or wireless options. Wired headphones often offer better sound quality at this price point.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you get good sound quality under $100?

Absolutely! Many reputable brands offer excellent sound quality in this price range. The key is choosing established brands known for audio quality rather than unknown brands with flashy marketing.

Should I choose wired or wireless under $100?

At this price point, wired headphones typically offer better sound quality and reliability. Wireless options are available but often sacrifice audio quality for the convenience of no cables.

How long do budget headphones last?

With proper care, budget headphones can last 2-5 years. Look for models with replaceable parts like ear cushions and cables for extended lifespan.
