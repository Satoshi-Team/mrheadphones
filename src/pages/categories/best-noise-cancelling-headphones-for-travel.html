Essential Features for Travel

Look for at least 20+ hours of battery life to cover long flights and travel days without needing to recharge frequently.

🎧 Comfort for Long Wear

Travel headphones should be comfortable enough to wear for hours during flights, train rides, or long commutes.

Travel can be rough on electronics. Look for sturdy construction and protective cases for your investment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much noise cancellation do I need for travel?

For air travel, you'll want active noise cancellation (ANC) that can reduce engine noise by 20-30dB. For trains and commuting, even basic ANC can make a significant difference.

Are over-ear or in-ear better for travel?

Over-ear headphones generally provide better noise cancellation and comfort for long flights, while in-ear options are more portable and better for active travel.

Can I use noise cancelling headphones during takeoff/landing?

Most airlines allow noise cancelling headphones during all phases of flight, but you may need to remove them during safety announcements.
