Essential Electrostatic Features

Ultra-Thin Diaphragm

Electrostatic headphones use diaphragms that are only a few microns thick, allowing for incredibly fast response times and the ability to reproduce the finest details in music.

Lightning-Fast Response

The electrostatic design provides instantaneous response to electrical signals, eliminating the inertia that affects other driver types and delivering unparalleled transient response.

Exceptional Transparency

Electrostatic headphones offer unmatched transparency, revealing every nuance and detail in the music with incredible accuracy and naturalness.

Special Amplification

Electrostatic headphones require specialized amplifiers called energizers that provide the high voltage needed to drive the electrostatic elements properly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are electrostatic headphones so expensive?

Electrostatic headphones require complex manufacturing processes, ultra-thin diaphragms, specialized amplifiers, and precision engineering, all of which contribute to their high cost.

Do electrostatic headphones need special amplification?

Yes, electrostatic headphones require specialized amplifiers called energizers that provide the high voltage (typically 500-600V) needed to drive the electrostatic elements.

Are electrostatic headphones worth the investment?

For serious audiophiles and audio professionals who demand the absolute best sound quality, electrostatic headphones offer unparalleled detail and transparency that justify their cost.
