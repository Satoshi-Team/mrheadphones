Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a gaming headset or can I use regular headphones?

While regular headphones can work, gaming headsets are optimized for gaming with features like surround sound, gaming-specific microphones, and comfort for long sessions.

Is wireless or wired better for gaming?

Wired headphones typically offer lower latency and no battery concerns, while wireless provides freedom of movement. For competitive gaming, wired is often preferred.

What's the difference between 7.1 and stereo gaming headphones?

7.1 surround sound provides directional audio cues for better spatial awareness, while stereo offers simpler, more accurate sound reproduction.
