What to Expect Under $200

ANC Performance

Budget ANC headphones typically reduce low-frequency noise by 70-85%, compared to 90%+ in premium models. Still very effective for most environments.

Build Quality

Expect mostly plastic construction with some metal accents. While not as premium as $300+ models, they're still durable for daily use.

Battery Life

Most budget ANC headphones offer 20-40 hours of battery life, which is often comparable to or better than premium options.

Sound Quality

Sound quality is generally good, though you may notice less detail and refinement compared to premium models. Still enjoyable for most music genres.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ANC worth it under $200?

Absolutely! While not as powerful as premium models, budget ANC headphones still provide significant noise reduction that can dramatically improve your listening experience.

How much ANC performance do I lose?

You typically lose 10-15% of ANC effectiveness compared to premium models, but this is still very effective for most everyday situations like commuting and office work.

Should I save up for premium ANC?

If you're on a budget, start with a good $200 option. You can always upgrade later, and many users find budget ANC headphones meet their needs perfectly.
